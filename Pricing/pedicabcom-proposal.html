Proposal of Work - Pedicab.com

This is a proposal of work to rebuild Pedicab.com as a modern site that works on all devices.

I'll be moving the site onto a WordPress platform to make upkeep easier and to improve the site's search engine optimization.

All pages of content will be restructured to be mobile-friendly and to fit into the new design.

I will make sure all content including videos, image galleries is moved over.

What This Package Includes

4 Rounds of Design Review

  • The new site's design will be mobile-friendly and modern. Looking good on mobile phones will improve the Google search ranking.

  • The site's design will match the style of the e-commerce store at shop.pedicab.com. This brand consistency will make the site easier to navigate.

  • Your feedback will be incorporated at every stage of the design and construction.

User Experience (UX) Consulting

  • Designing the User Experience means identifying the goals of your website and guiding the visitor towards completion.
  • I'll use UX design principles to strategically place content, purchase buttons and lead generation forms.

Unlimited Pages of Content

  • Pedicab.com has at least 18 pages of content right now which will need to be moved to the new mobile-friendly platform.
  • Although the pages are mostly written, I'll need to completely re-organize the images and content into a mobile-friendly layout for each page.
  • I'll also need to create new pages for each of the PDFs I'm converting.

SEO Best Practices on Each Page

  • Every page on the new site will be optimized for Google's search engines - title tags, keywords, etc.
  • I will also install a tool on the site that will monitor each page's SEO score and alert to any problems.

Custom Emails @yoursite.com

  • You already have custom emails set up, this won't disrupt them.
  • I'll include an extra Premium Feature in place of this work item.

Up to 3 Premium Features

  • Custom Homepage Slider
  • Image Gallery Page
  • Video Gallery Page - might require video conversion
  • SalesForce Form Integration
  • Custom Page Designs - Operators, Advertising, Pedicabs, etc.
  • PDF conversion to webpages

I'm including all 6 Premium Features above at no extra charge.
